(ABout me)

Jahseh grew up in a small city in Florida called Broward County. His interest in music began early in his teen years, at around 13 years old. Because of the divorce with his parents when he was about 2 years of age, Jahseh was always a troubled kid. He got into a lot of trouble while attending school, due to fights and trouble with teachers. He also got into trouble with the law a few times, due to robbery, drugs, etc. This ended up getting him into a Juvenile Detention Center, where he met friend and rapper Ski Mask the Slump God. Jahseh dropped out of high school to pursue his music career, along side Ski Mask..
